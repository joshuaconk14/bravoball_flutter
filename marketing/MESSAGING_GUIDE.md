## üîÑ Message Testing

### A/B Test Ideas

**Value Proposition Variations**:
- "Train like a pro" vs. "Professional training"
- "AI-powered" vs. "Personalized"
- "Free to try" vs. "Start free"

**CTA Variations**:
- "Download now" vs. "Start training"
- "Get started" vs. "Try free"
- "Improve your game" vs. "Start training"

### Testing Framework

1. **Hypothesis**: What message will perform better?
2. **Test**: Run A/B test with equal traffic
3. **Measure**: Track CTR, conversion, engagement
4. **Learn**: Document results and insights
5. **Iterate**: Apply learnings to future campaigns
